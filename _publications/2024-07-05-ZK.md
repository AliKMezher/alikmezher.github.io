### Abstract
[
This paper is focused on the modified Zakharov-Kusnetsov equation

\begin{equation}
\label{abstmZK}
    \pt_t u(t,x,y) + \pt_x^3 u(t,x,y) + \pt_x u^3(t,x,y)=0.
\end{equation}

We prove that the associated Cauchy problem is locally (in time) well-posed in $H^s(\R \times \T)$ for $s > 1$. The new ingredient in this work is a trilinear estimate in the context of Bourgain spaces, controlling the cubic nonlinearity of the modified Zakharov-Kuznetsov equation that naturally arises in the contraction argument.

\textit{Keywords:} Nonlinear Strichatz estimates, modified Zakharov-Kuznetsov equation, harmonic analysis.
.]
